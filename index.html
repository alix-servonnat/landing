Origins of a passion

It was when my family and I adopted a little Maltese we called Jimmy that I fell in love with this breed of dog. Docile, loving and intelligent, these little creatures improve their owners' daily lives while remaining very discreet thanks to their size. True companions, once they've adopted you as their master they never let you go, listening to your every move like little guardian angels. For lonely people, the Maltese is the perfect companion, even when traveling by car, plane or boat...
